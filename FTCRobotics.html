Robotics


FTC 14020 is a First Tech Challenge Robotics team based in Robbinsville High School We are 15 dedicated members who work together to create a robot designed to accomplish tasks in a different challenge each year. We strive to fun ourselves through marketing, sponsors, and grants. We also strive to serve the community and teach them about important STEM skills in the 21st century, spreading the message of the FIRST organization.
