Robotics


FTC 14020 is a First Tech Challenge Robotics team based in Robbinsville High School. I was a member of FTC 14020 from 2018-2021. The organization has taught me many lessons about teamwork, engineering, and leadership. It has served as a challenge of many of my own skills, and my work for the team consists of some of my proudest engineering accomplishments yet.
