COSU

The Corps of Cadets Cyber Operations Special Unit (COSU), taught me a lot during my Sophomore year, and since then I have earned the title of first sergeant for the unit, helping them plan and carry out lessons for old and new members, as well as preparing the unit for competions like the NCX. It has been an incredible experience to grow my ability to understand cybersecurity and teach it to others.

Defense Cyber Leadership Development Program

The Defense Cyber Leadership Development Program, or DCLDP, is a program sponsored by the DOD dedicated to helping students prepare for and get into Cybersecurity positions within the DOD. DCLDP provides students with the resources and experiences they need to become Cybersecurity profesionals, connecting us with mentorys, helping us earn certifications, as well as an annual trip to DEFCON. Learn more about DCLCDP below.

TAMU Security Operations Center

As part of my membership with the Defense Cyber Leaderhsip Development Program, I am a student worker at the Texas A&M System Cybersecurity Operations Center. I investigate potentially malicious activiy occuring on the networks of several Texas A&M campuses.
